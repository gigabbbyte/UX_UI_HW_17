Hello,
I'm Gabby!

A lover of details big and small, I am a UI/UX and visual designer who can’t help but think of ways I can help improve the human experience. Whether it’s with user interviews or the placement of a button where one wasn’t, I aim to make products not only easy to experience, but delightful, too.



When I'm not designing, you can find me looking at pictures of Snoopy, or in the DVD section of Goodwill listening to my iPod and living out my y2k dream life!

What do I know?


User Research


The most important lesson I’ve learned is to ask “why,” and then ask it four more times. Through careful observation, surveys, and interviews, I delve into the user's mindset to understand their needs and preferences.


Prototyping


Prototyping is my favorite part of the design process because it marks the amalgamation of user research and design ideas. Sketching layouts, creating components, and bringing prototypes to life through interaction and animation are my favorite parts of transitioning from concepts to tangible products.


Web Development


While front-end web development is technically challenging, it satisfies my always-curious design brain. I leverage this curiosity and attention to detail to create responsive websites with the help of CSS, HTML, and Bootstrap.
